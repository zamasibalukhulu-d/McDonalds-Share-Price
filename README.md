# McDonalds-Share-Price
A historical data analysis on  McDonald's Share Price from 2000 to 2025

***DATA SOURCE***: @ Yahoo Fianance

## Data Description:
Dataset contains share price data from the year 2000 to 2005 
**Key Idenfifiers:**
- Close: Final closing trading price of the stock before market closing
- High: The highest price that the stock reached at trading day
- Low: The lowest price that the stock dropped from the initial price.
